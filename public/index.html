You receive a complete set of screens for your product — every view, every state, every component — all designed with a fully consistent visual language. You can regenerate as many times as you need within your monthly kit allowance. The more detail you provide in your description — genre, tone, color preferences, reference products — the closer UXLora gets on the first attempt. Every kit is built specifically around your input, not from a generic template library.

UXLora works for mobile games, mobile apps, and web products equally. Whether you need a game HUD and inventory system, a mobile app onboarding flow, or a SaaS dashboard — UXLora understands the context and generates screens appropriate for that product type.
